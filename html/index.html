In the modern interior of Ukrainian homes, abstraction still gives way to traditional genres of fine art, such as landscape and still life. However, every year avant-garde paintings are gaining more and more popularity. The reasons for the gradual shift in trends are quite clear - the modern pace of life significantly changes our view of the world, and with it our perception of beauty.
